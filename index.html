Projects

  • Samvad - Bridging Voices, Connecting Lives - Samvad, meaning communication in Sanskrit, is a project designed to bridge the communication gap for individuals with hearing and speech impairments. It offers real-time voice-to-text and text-to-voice translation, with a focus on supporting Indian regional languages such as Hindi, Marathi, Tamil, and more. By enabling users to convert spoken words into text or text into speech instantly, Samvad empowers people to communicate effectively in various settings, including education, workplaces, and daily life. This inclusive solution promotes accessibility, independence, and linguistic diversity, making it an invaluable tool for breaking barriers and fostering meaningful interactions for those with communication challenges.

  • SMART - Schedule Management and Academic Reminder Tool - SMART is a web-based application designed to streamline administrative tasks for faculty and staff. This tool allows users to effortlessly create, manage, and download customized academic calendars based on key inputs such as semester start and end dates, holidays, and exam schedules. It automates the process of generating departmental documents, ensuring consistency and reducing manual errors. Additionally, the integrated reminder system ensures timely notifications for important events, deadlines, and meetings, helping faculty and staff stay organized and productive. By simplifying calendar management, this project enhances efficiency, saves time, and improves overall planning within academic institutions.
